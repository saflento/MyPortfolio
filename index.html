Projects

Student Course Registration Coordination System (Final Year Project)

The Student Course Registration Coordination System (SCRCS) is a mobile app facilitating efficient interaction among students, faculty course registration administrators, and lecturers to address course registration concerns. It features a tripartite chat box, divided into course registration issues, technical problems, and faculty selection, enhancing issue-specific communication. Students can log in, report specific issues, and directly engage with the course's program coordinator and lecturers in a dedicated chat. System admins delegate roles to lecturers, such as program coordination, teaching, or troubleshooting, increasing efficiency in problem-solving. Lecturers can swiftly inform students about updates or changes like quota increase timings through the chat box. For technical issues, students can log a ticket, decreasing resolution times and ensuring a smooth registration process.


Computer System Administration and Management course

My team members and I worked on deploying the Netdata real-time health monitoring and performance tool for Linux systems. I familiarized myself with its functionalities and installed and configured the tool on a Ubuntu-based system. I evaluated its utility in two scenarios: Security Management and Performance Management, demonstrating its capacity to detect and mitigate a DOS attack and monitor the system during a stress test.


Web-Based System Development Course

My team members and I developed a Meeting Room Booking and Management System from scratch, without the use of any content management systems or front-end/back-end frameworks. This application supported features such as user account registration and management, admin control over CRUD operations, and a dashboard for users to view booking details, make payments, and receive reports. Additionally, the system was capable of generating and auto-sending payment receipts or invoices to the user's email address. I utilized HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP for web development and MySQL for database management.
